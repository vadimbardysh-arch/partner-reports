📊 Overview

Smart Promo Topup Low / Med / High traits segment providers by topup level for Smart Promo campaigns. Providers in these traits receive offers via the Provider Portal where they can activate campaigns with various discount & co-financing levels.

🎯 SP Offers

Double Deal bundles combine Menu Discount + Free Delivery campaigns. Regular offers are single campaigns. Cost Share 50% = provider pays 50% of the discount. 100% Bolt = fully funded by Bolt.
